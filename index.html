We Are Vectors

In this talk series, we will explore identity and how it impacts work, communities, and the world at large. We'll discuss the interwoven characteristics of creative work and daily life with some of the most innovative minds in the industry.

At Vectors, you’ll connect with designers from different backgrounds who will share how they've used their perspective as a superpower. In turn, we hope you leave the event inspired to leverage what is uniquely you.

Series

  • Creative Confidence

    In this talk, Pablo will share insights about how being vulnerable and bringing your shield down can help you at being more creative and authentic. The conversation touches on leaving perfectionism behind and overcoming the fear of portraying weakness to find joy in your work.

    Pablo Stanley

    Product Designer, Carbon Health

  • Sight to See

    What if the problem you were solving was tied directly to your identity? This is a talk about handling the responsibility of objectively making important design decisions for your own community knowing damn well that the stakes are high.

    Robyn Kanner

    Co-Founder, MyTransHealth

  • Untapped Potential

    A discussion centered around hiring, retention, and directive. Panelists will discuss how to send the right signals to build a diverse team and what to do afterward to keep it that way.

    Dana Lawson

    VP of Engineering, InvisionApp

    Jacinda Walker

    Founder, designExplorr

    Cathy Lo

    Product Designer, Instagram

    Chikezie Ejiasi

    Product Designer, Google

    Cheryl Cheng

    Blue Run Ventures

  • Chaos to Craft

    Seemingly disperse background, interests, and skills can appear conflicting at first, but one designer's journey leads them to embrace the chaos to improve his craft.

    Alex Valdivia

    Interactive Designer, Blogger

  • Designing for Unconscious Bias

    In this talk, Rafa will discuss how unconscious bias shows up in everyday interactions as well as in systems that shape our lives, and will highlight several opportunities to use design and tech to mitigate its negative influences.

    Rafael Smith

    Design Lead, IDEO.org

  • Techies Portrait Series

    Become a part of the Techies family with tech’s favorite photographer, Helena Price. This interactive installation is a once in a lifetime opportunity to share your story and join the Techies tribe.

    Helena Price

    Independent Photographer
